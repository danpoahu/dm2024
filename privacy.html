Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Information We Collect

Discover More collects the following information when you create an account: your name, email address, and optionally your phone number. We also store your survey responses (DISC personality and Spiritual Gifts assessments) to provide your results.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the Discover More experience, including generating your personality and spiritual gifts results, and helping Anchor Church connect you with serving opportunities that match your gifts.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties outside of Anchor Church ministry purposes.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Profile section of the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@discovermore.app.
